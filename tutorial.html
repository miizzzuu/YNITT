How to Sign Up
for Facebook?

Follow the instructions below!

Step 1: Go to the Facebook Website and Sign Up

1. On the Facebook homepage, you'll see a section titled "Create a New Account."
2. Fill out the following fields.

Step 2: Click the "Sign Up" Button

After filling out all the fields, click the green "Sign Up" button at the bottom of the form.

Step 3: Confirm Your Email or Mobile Number

1. Facebook will send a confirmation code to the email address or mobile number you provided.
2. Enter the confirmation code in the provided field and click Continue.

Step 4: Set Up Your Profile

1. Profile Picture, you can either upload a photo or take a new one using your camera.
2. Complete Your Profile: You can add more information to your profile, such as your work, education, hometown, and more.

Congratulations!

You have successfully signed up for Facebook. Enjoy connecting with friends and family!
